DSR started its journey in 2017 to serve clients with Renewable Solar Energy Solutions including Photovoltaic, Thermal & Solar Appliances. Today, DSR has emerged as a very prominent Solar PV EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) Player to provide comprehensive turnkey solutions to its clients in all three spheres of Domestic, Commercial & Industrial Segment. DSR offers the PV solution starting from 1 kWp to Megawatt range. Along-with ground mounted grid connected Solar PV plant & roof top Off Grid or Grid Interactive System, we also offer Solar Pump & Appliances.

DSR has a large team of 25+ Engineers & Technicians to cater to your requirement.

Our wide network across India consists of nearly 40 Dealers & Distributors. With best of engineering practices, DSR sources the most efficient & reliable equipment to ensure highest power generation for the entire life of the SPV Plant.
